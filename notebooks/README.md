# Notebooks

Colab-targeted Jupyter notebooks for data preparation, experimentation, and training.

## Usage

1. Open a notebook in [Google Colab](https://colab.research.google.com/).
2. Follow the instructions in the first cell.
3. Results and artifacts are saved to Google Drive at `MyDrive/DL-Project/`.
